# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We commit to creating an open, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and collaborate professionally.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or doxxing.
- Publishing private/sensitive data without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict
participation for violations of this code.
